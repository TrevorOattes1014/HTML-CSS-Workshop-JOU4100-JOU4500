Welcome!

Welcome to my homepage, where I primarily share photos of parrots that I took in the summer of 2019. Parrots are violent and wrathful creatures, so the purpose of this website is less to enjoy the pretty feathers but moreso to act as a warning to anyone foolish enough to approach these monsters. Never, ever forget that these hellspawn are the direct descendants of dinosaurs.

Photo Work:

Here we have Bernie. While Bernie certainly seems sweet and cuddly, on the inside he is a furious ball of carnage the likes have which could only be rivaled by a biblical plague. Behind those amber eyes lies an unquenchable and absolute disdain for all things human.

The job of feeding Bernie and the other birds within this room often fell to me and thus I have an intimate familiarity with this feathered felon. Bernie was talkative even among the other parrots, and would often say - after staring with those empty eyes for discomfortingly long time - "wanna play?" He would then unleash a wave of kamikaze strikes with the sole intention of forcing me out of the room. This bird has hurt me more than most people, and that is no small task.
